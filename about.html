Biography

Born in Nepal and raised in Kathmandu, Prasanna Pradhan is a full-time student with a love for photography, videography, music, and visual arts. Inspired by his grandfather and supported by his single mother, Prasanna has overcome many challenges. His passion for creativity led him to become a self-taught photographer and an active participant in the Porsche Club. Looking forward, he aims to become a professional photographer, sell his artwork, produce music, and pursue a career in cybersecurity as a penetration tester. Every day, he finds motivation in new experiences and the opportunity to grow creatively and intellectually.
